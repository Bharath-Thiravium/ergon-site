# User Manual: Salary Advance & Loans

## 7.1 Overview
The Advance module enables employees to request financial advances against their salary, subject to organizational eligibility rules.

## 7.2 Eligibility Check
The system automatically calculates eligibility based on:
- Current salary.
- Existing active loans/advances.
- Pending repayment schedules.

*If you are ineligible, the "Request Advance" button may be disabled.*

## 7.3 Requesting an Advance
**Procedure:**
1. Navigate to **Advance / Loans** in the menu.
2. Click **New Request**.
3. Enter the **Amount** required.
4. Select the **Repayment Period** (e.g., 1 month, 3 months).
   - *The system will show the estimated monthly deduction.*
5. Enter the **Reason** for the request.
6. Click **Submit**.

## 7.4 Tracking Status
1. Go to **Advance > My Requests**.
2. The status column indicates:
   - **Pending:** Awaiting HR/Finance approval.
   - **Approved:** Request accepted; funds disbursement in progress.
   - **Disbursed:** Funds transferred to your account.
   - **Rejected:** Request denied (check comments for details).

## 7.5 Repayment History
To view how much has been deducted from your salary:
1. Navigate to **Advance > Repayment Schedule**.
2. View the list of past and upcoming deductions.

## 7.6 Types of Advances

- **Salary Advance:** Short-term cash advance.
- **Loan:** Longer-term financing.
- **Emergency Advance:** For urgent needs.

## 7.7 Interest and Fees

- Interest rates vary by type and amount.
- Processing fees may apply.

## 7.8 Approval Process

- Auto-approval for small amounts.
- Manager/Finance approval for larger requests.

## 7.9 Disbursement Methods

- Direct bank transfer.
- Payroll inclusion.

## 7.10 Reporting

View advance reports and repayment status.

## 7.11 Integration with Payroll

Automatic deductions from salary.

## 7.12 Policies

- Maximum advance: 50% of monthly salary.
- Minimum repayment: 3 months.
- No advances for probationary employees.

## 7.13 Mobile Access

Request advances via mobile app.

## 7.14 Notifications

Alerts for approval, disbursement, and repayments.

## 7.15 Troubleshooting

**Q: Request rejected.**  
A: Check eligibility or contact Finance.

**Q: Deductions not showing.**  
A: Verify with payroll.
