# User Manual: Attendance Tracking

## 8.1 Overview
The Attendance module records daily work hours, check-in/check-out times, and attendance status (Present, Absent, Late).

## 8.2 Marking Attendance (Web Clock-In)
*Note: This feature depends on your organization's configuration (Biometric vs. Web).*

**Procedure:**
1. Log in to the Dashboard.
2. Locate the **Attendance Widget** (usually top right or main dashboard).
3. Click **Clock In** at the start of the day.
   - *System records the server time.*
4. Click **Clock Out** at the end of the day.

## 8.3 Viewing Attendance Logs
1. Navigate to **Attendance > My Attendance**.
2. Select the **Month** and **Year**.
3. The calendar view displays:
   - **Green:** Present / On Time.
   - **Red:** Absent.
   - **Yellow:** Late Arrival / Early Departure.
   - **Grey:** Weekend / Holiday.

## 8.4 Regularization (Correction Request)
If you forgot to clock in or had a biometric issue:

1. Go to **Attendance > Regularization**.
2. Click **Request Correction**.
3. Select the **Date** of the discrepancy.
4. Enter the correct **In Time** and **Out Time**.
5. Provide a **Reason** (e.g., "Forgot ID card", "Scanner Error").
6. Click **Submit** for manager approval.
