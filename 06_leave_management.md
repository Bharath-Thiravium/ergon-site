# User Manual: Leave Management

## 6.1 Overview
The Leave Management module allows employees to apply for time off, view their leave balances, and track the status of their requests.

## 6.2 Checking Leave Balance
Before applying, it is recommended to check your available leave credits.

1. Click on **Leave** in the main navigation menu.
2. Select **My Leave Balance**.
3. The system displays a summary table showing:
   - **Leave Type** (Casual, Sick, Earned, etc.)
   - **Total Allocated**
   - **Used**
   - **Remaining Balance**

## 6.3 Applying for Leave
**Procedure:**
1. Navigate to **Leave > Apply for Leave**.
2. Fill in the application form:
   - **Leave Type:** Select the appropriate category from the dropdown.
   - **Duration:** Choose "Full Day" or "Half Day".
   - **Start Date & End Date:** Select the range from the calendar.
   - **Reason:** Provide a brief explanation for the approver.
   - **Reliever (Optional):** Select a colleague who will cover your duties.
3. Click **Submit Request**.

**Expected Outcome:**
- The request status becomes **"Pending"**.
- An email notification is sent to your reporting manager.

## 6.4 Canceling a Leave Request
You can cancel a request only if it has not yet been approved (or depending on company policy).

1. Go to **Leave > My Leave History**.
2. Locate the pending request.
3. Click the **Cancel** button (X icon).
4. Confirm the cancellation.

## 6.5 Approval Workflow (For Managers)
If you are a manager:
1. Navigate to **Leave > Team Requests**.
2. Review the pending applications.
3. Click **Approve** or **Reject**.
4. If rejecting, you may be required to enter a reason.

## 6.6 Types of Leave

- **Annual Leave:** Paid time off accrued annually.
- **Sick Leave:** For medical reasons.
- **Casual Leave:** Short-term personal leave.
- **Maternity/Paternity Leave:** For new parents.
- **Emergency Leave:** Unplanned absences.

## 6.7 Leave Policies

- Minimum notice period: 2 weeks for annual leave.
- Maximum consecutive days: 30 days.
- Carry-over: Up to 10 days to next year.
- Blackout dates: No leave during peak seasons.

## 6.8 Leave Calendar

View team leave calendar to plan around absences.

## 6.9 Bulk Approvals

Managers can approve multiple requests at once.

## 6.10 Reporting

Generate leave reports for HR analysis.

## 6.11 Integration with Payroll

Approved leaves automatically update payroll calculations.

## 6.12 Mobile Features

Apply and approve leaves on mobile.

## 6.13 Notifications

Automated reminders for upcoming leaves or approvals.

## 6.14 Troubleshooting

**Q: Request stuck in pending.**  
A: Check with your manager.

**Q: Balance not updating.**  
A: Contact HR.

**Q: Unable to apply.**  
A: Ensure sufficient balance.
