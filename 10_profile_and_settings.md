# User Manual: Profile & Settings

## 10.1 Overview
The Profile section allows users to manage their personal information, security settings, and notification preferences.

## 10.2 Editing Personal Information
1. Click on your **Avatar/Name** in the top right corner.
2. Select **My Profile**.
3. Click **Edit Profile**.
4. You can update allowed fields such as:
   - Phone Number
   - Emergency Contact
   - Current Address
   - Profile Picture
5. Click **Save Changes**.

*Note: Critical fields like Email, Designation, and Department are usually locked and managed by HR.*

## 10.3 Changing Password
For security, update your password regularly.

1. Navigate to **Profile > Security** or **Change Password**.
2. Enter your **Current Password**.
3. Enter the **New Password** (ensure it meets complexity requirements).
4. Confirm the **New Password**.
5. Click **Update Password**.

## 10.4 Notification Settings
Control how the system alerts you.

1. Go to **Settings > Notifications**.
2. Toggle preferences for:
   - Email Alerts (New Tasks, Leave Approvals).
   - Browser Push Notifications.
3. Click **Save Preferences**.
