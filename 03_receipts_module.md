# User Manual: Receipts Module

## 3.1 Overview

The Receipts module allows employees to submit expense receipts for reimbursement. It supports various expense categories and includes approval workflows.

## 3.2 Supported Expense Types

- Travel expenses (flights, hotels, transportation)
- Meal allowances
- Office supplies
- Client entertainment
- Miscellaneous expenses

## 3.3 Submitting a Receipt

**Procedure:**
1. Navigate to **Expenses > Submit Receipt** in the main menu.
2. Click **New Expense Claim**.
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Expense Date:** Date of the expense.
   - **Category:** Select from dropdown (e.g., Travel, Meals).
   - **Amount:** Enter the expense amount.
   - **Description:** Brief explanation.
   - **Receipt Upload:** Attach digital copy of the receipt (PDF, JPG, PNG up to 5MB).
4. Click **Save** to add more items or **Submit** to send for approval.

**Expected Outcome:**
- The claim status becomes **Pending Approval**.
- Email notification sent to your manager.

## 3.4 Viewing Expense History

1. Go to **Expenses > My Expenses**.
2. Filter by date range or status.
3. Click on a claim to view details, including attached receipts.

## 3.5 Approval Process (For Managers)

1. Navigate to **Expenses > Team Approvals**.
2. Review pending claims.
3. Click **Approve** or **Reject**.
4. For rejections, provide a reason.
5. Approved claims are forwarded to Finance for reimbursement.

## 3.6 Reimbursement Tracking

Once approved:
1. Go to **Expenses > Reimbursements**.
2. View payment status and expected date.
3. Reimbursements are typically processed via payroll or direct deposit.

## 3.7 Receipt Upload Guidelines

- File formats: PDF, JPG, PNG
- Maximum file size: 5MB per file
- Multiple receipts can be attached to one claim
- Ensure receipts are legible and include date, amount, and vendor details

## 3.8 Expense Policies

- All expenses must be business-related.
- Keep original receipts for audit purposes.
- Submit claims within 30 days of the expense.
- Pre-approval required for expenses over $500.

## 3.9 Reporting

Generate expense reports from **Expenses > Reports** for personal or team analysis.

## 3.10 Bulk Expense Submission

For multiple expenses:
1. Use the **Bulk Upload** feature.
2. Download the CSV template.
3. Fill in expense details.
4. Upload the CSV file.
5. Review and submit.

## 3.11 Mileage and Per Diem

- **Mileage:** Enter distance and rate for vehicle expenses.
- **Per Diem:** Automatic calculation based on location and duration.

## 3.12 Tax Implications

The system calculates applicable taxes. Consult Finance for details.

## 3.13 Integration with Accounting Software

Expenses sync with QuickBooks or SAP for seamless processing.

## 3.14 Approval Workflows

- Single approver for small amounts.
- Multi-level approval for large expenses.
- Automatic routing based on amount thresholds.

## 3.15 Notifications

- Email alerts for submission, approval, rejection.
- In-app notifications for status updates.

## 3.16 Audit Trail

All changes are logged for compliance and auditing.

## 3.17 Mobile App Features

Submit receipts on-the-go using the mobile app with camera integration.

## 3.18 Troubleshooting

**Q: Receipt upload fails.**  
A: Check file size and format.

**Q: Claim rejected.**  
A: Review the reason and resubmit with corrections.

**Q: Reimbursement delayed.**  
A: Contact Finance department.
